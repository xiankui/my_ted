## Resilience in Capital

Given the extreme speed of the market, a small bug in a trading algorithm can lead to a disaster. However, a truly advanced system is not one which never fails, but one which can "self-correct" in real-time.

In terms of high-frequency trading, success all comes down to risk management. You must build "circuit breakers" into your logic to get away from emotional panic or technical crashes. This is a digital form of resilience. Just like humans, the best algorithms are those that learn from their errors and adapt to new data. In this world, adaptability is the name of the game.

## 1. 核心骨架 (The Core)

文中有一句：“The best algorithms are those that learn from their errors...”

骨架： The best algorithms are those. (最好的算法是那些 [算法])

标签： ...that learn from their errors and adapt to new data. (那些能从错误中学习并适应新数据的 [算法])

## 2. 逻辑锚点与核能动词

Circuit breaker：断路器/保险丝。（在金融里指“熔断机制”，在心理学里指“强制让自己冷静下来的停顿”）

Self-correct：自我修正。（AI 和交易系统最核心的进化能力）

Adapt to：适应。（从心理韧性到算法进化的终极目标）
