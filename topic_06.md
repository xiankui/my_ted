# The Art of Slow Living

Given the fast-paced world we live in, we often feel like we are running a race with no finish line. We try to automate everything to save time, but then we use that time to do even more work.

In terms of mental health, "Slow Living" is not about doing nothing. It’s about being intentional. It all comes down to quality over quantity. By slowing down, we can notice the details which we usually ignore. In the end, protecting your inner peace is the name of the game.

## 1. 核心骨架 (The Core)

文中有一句：“We can notice the details which we usually ignore.”

* 骨架： We can notice the details. (我们可以注意到细节)

* 标签： ...which we usually ignore. (那些我们通常忽略的 [细节])

## 2. 逻辑锚点与核能动词

* Fast-paced world：快节奏的世界。（描述现代生活的万能背景词）

* Being intentional：有意识地、故意的。（这是心理学中的高阶词汇，指你不被生活推着走，而是自己决定做什么）

* Filter out (潜台词)：当我们太快时，大脑会过滤掉（filter out）快乐；只有慢下来，才能重新接收信号。
