# The FOMO Trap and Reverse Thinking

Given the madness of the crowd, FOMO is the biggest enemy of your wallet. When a stock price rockets, you feel a "spotlight" on your empty hands. This pressure forces you to buy at the top.

However, professional investors use reverse thinking. In terms of market cycles, what goes up must come down. Instead of following the heat, they focus on the "intrinsic value" which remains stable. It all comes down to emotional control. If you can get away from the noise and wait for the right moment, patience is the name of the game.

## 1. 核心骨架 (The Core)

文中有一句：“They focus on the 'intrinsic value' which remains stable.”

骨架： They focus on the "intrinsic value". (他们关注“内在价值”)

标签： ...which remains stable. (那些保持稳定的 [内在价值])

## 2. 逻辑锚点与核能动词

Given the madness of the crowd：考虑到人群的疯狂。（设定了动荡的市场背景）

Intrinsic value：内在价值。（金融学核心词汇，指公司真正的值钱程度，而非股价）

What goes up must come down：凡是涨上去的必会跌下来。（经典的金融谚语，也是一种思维模型）
