# Price vs. Intrinsic Value

Given that the market is like a moody person, prices change every second. However, price is not the same as value. In terms of smart investing, you should look for the "intrinsic value" which is the real worth of a business.

Think of it like buying a house. The market price might drop because of a bad mood, but the house itself (the rooms, the location, the structure) stays the same. It all comes down to the cash flow that the business can generate in the future. If you can get through the market panic and buy below the value, you are winning.

## 1. 核心骨架 (The Core)

文中有一句：“It all comes down to the cash flow that the business can generate in the future.”

骨架： It all comes down to the cash flow. (一切归结于现金流)

标签： ...that the business can generate in the future. (企业未来能产生的 [现金流])

## 2. 核心词汇与动词

Cash Flow (现金流)：这是金融学的“氧气”，指企业实际赚进来的钱。

Generate (产生/创造)：比 make 更正式，常用于产生利润、电力或想法。

Get through (度过/穿越)：我们在第一章学过的核能动词。Get through the panic 意味着在恐慌中保持冷静。
