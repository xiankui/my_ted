# The Art of Forgetting

Given the limit of our memory, forgetting is actually a survival skill. Our brains are designed to filter out "noise" that we don't use. If you only memorize a word list once, your brain thinks it's trash and deletes it.

In terms of long-term memory, the secret is not "hard work," but "smart timing." Instead of fighting against forgetting, we should embrace it. By meeting a word in different stories or videos, we tell our brains: "This is important!" Eventually, the "noise" becomes a "signal" which stays with us forever.
