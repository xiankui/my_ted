# The Science of Mindfulness

Given the constant distractions of digital life, our attention is often fragmented. We spend so much time worrying about the future or regretting the past.

In terms of brain health, Mindfulness is the practice of returning to the "now." It all comes down to being an observer of your own thoughts. Instead of being swept away by emotions, you simply notice them as "clouds" which pass through the sky. By training your brain to stay present, you can get away from unnecessary stress. After all, clarity is the name of the game.

## 1. 核心骨架 (The Core)

文中有一句：“You simply notice them as 'clouds' which pass through the sky.”

骨架： You notice them as "clouds". (你把它们[情绪]看作云)

标签： ...which pass through the sky. (那些穿过天空的 [云])

## 2. 逻辑锚点与核能动词

Fragmented：破碎的、碎片化的。（描述现代人注意力的精准词汇）

Stay present：保持在当下。（正念的核心：不念过去，不畏将来）

Swept away：被卷走。（形容被情绪带节奏的生动表达）
