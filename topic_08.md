# Building Your Emotional Resilience

Given the unpredictable nature of life, we cannot avoid stress, but we can change how we respond to it. Many people think resilience is about being "tough," but in terms of psychology, it is actually about flexibility.

It all comes down to your "internal dialogue"—the way you talk to yourself. Instead of saying "I failed," resilient people say "I am learning." This is a growth mindset which turns every obstacle into a stepping stone. By reframing your challenges, you can get through the darkest moments and come out stronger. In the end, mindset is the name of the game.

## 1. 核心骨架 (The Core)

文中有一句：“This is a growth mindset which turns every obstacle into a stepping stone.”

骨架： This is a growth mindset. (这是一种成长型思维)

标签： ...which turns every obstacle into a stepping stone. (那些能把障碍变成垫脚石的 [思维])

## 2. 逻辑锚点与核能动词

Unpredictable nature：不可预测的本质。（描述市场或生活的常用背景）

Bounce back：回弹/恢复。（韧性的核心动作）

Reframing：重新框架/换个角度看问题。（心理调节中最有力的技术）

Stepping stone：垫脚石。（比喻成功的助推力）
