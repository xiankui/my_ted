# Why AI Makes Us More Human

Given the rapid rise of AI, many people feel anxious about their future. They fear that robots will take their jobs. However, if we shift our focus, we can see a different story.

It all comes down to one thing: creativity. AI is great at processing data, but it lacks the "human spark" which drives true innovation. By letting AI handle the boring tasks, we can get away from repetitive work and focus on what we are best at. In the end, being human is the name of the game.


## 逻辑拆解 (The Breakdown)

1. 核心骨架 (The Core)

文中有一句：“AI lacks the 'human spark' which drives true innovation.”

骨架： AI lacks the "human spark". (AI 缺少“人类火花”)

标签： ...which drives true innovation. (驱动真正创新的 [火花])

2. 逻辑锚点与核能动词

Given...：设定了“AI 崛起”的大背景。

Shift our focus：改变我们的视角（从恐惧转向观察）。

Get away from：从枯燥重复的工作中“逃离”出来。


## 微型挑战 (Mini Challenge)

在这篇文章中，我用了一句：


**By letting AI handle the boring tasks, we can get away from repetitive work...**


现在轮到你了：
假设你想告诉你的朋友：“通过使用 AI 绘图（AI drawing），我可以摆脱（get away from）那种枯燥的修改工作。”
